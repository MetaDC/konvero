What is a Konvero Fellow?

A Fellow is a registered and verified member of Konvero – The Conversation Club. Fellows are healthcare professionals or service providers committed to ethical collaboration, case-based learning, and cross-disciplinary conversations.

FAQs:

  • Q: Who qualifies as a Fellow?
    A: Verified healthcare professionals or vendors meeting Konvero’s eligibility criteria.

  • Q: Are there different types of Fellows?
    A: Yes – Core Fellow (founding member), Fellow (active member), and Guest Fellow (visiting prospect).
Own Your KONVERO Circle

Fellowship Overview & FAQs

What is a Circle at Konvero?

A Circle is a curated group of 40–50 verified healthcare professionals who meet twice a month to engage in structured, ethical, and collaborative learning. These sessions include introductions, presentations, discussions, and guest talks—designed to foster professional growth and build trust-based networks within healthcare.

Fellowship Categories

  • PC Fellows: Doctors, therapists, paramedics, and hospital owners involved in patient care.
  • NPC Fellows: Vendors, service providers, and consultants with at least 50% of their clientele in healthcare.

    • All Fellows are verified and categorized based on their role in the ecosystem.

What Happens at Konvero Club?

Konvero Club is a non-commercial platform for ethical conversations, interdisciplinary learning, and clinical networking. Circle meets are held twice a month for 90 minutes and feature structured segments like Capsule introductions, Canvas presentations, case discussions, solution showcases, and expert sessions.

Presentations by Fellows

  • Capsule: A 60-second intro with referral and learning takeaway.
  • Canvas: A 10-minute deep dive into their clinical or service practice.

    • NPC Fellows can share Solution Showcases, and all meets may feature invited guest experts for additional value.

Circle Leadership (Lead Host Team)

Each Circle is led by a Host Team, including:

  • Chair & Vice Chair: Elected by Fellows.
  • Admin Fellow: Nominated to support logistics.
  • Circle Guide: A Konvero-appointed team member. They ensure smooth operations, coordinate sessions, and foster collaboration among Fellows.

Expression of Interest

Applications to join a Circle open from 25th June 2025. The process includes submitting an Expression of Interest (EOI), followed by document verification, referral checks, approval, and payment of the privilege fee. Fellows may indicate Circle preferences during this stage.
